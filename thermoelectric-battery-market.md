<p><ul><li>Single Stage Module</li><li>Multiple Modules</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The thermoelectric battery market is categorized into single-stage and multiple modules. Single-stage modules utilize a singular thermoelectric effect process, generating power from a single temperature difference, making them compact and cost-effective for small-scale applications. In contrast, multiple modules consist of interconnected thermoelectric elements, enhancing efficiency and power output by leveraging greater temperature gradients. This type is ideal for larger applications needing substantial energy conversion, such as industrial systems and waste heat recovery, maximizing performance in diverse settings.</p></p>
