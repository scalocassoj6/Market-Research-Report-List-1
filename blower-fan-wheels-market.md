<p><ul><li>Backward Curve Fan Wheels</li><li>Forward Curve Fan Wheels</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blower fan wheels are categorized primarily into backward curve and forward curve designs, each serving distinct applications. Backward curve fan wheels feature curved blades that face away from the direction of rotation, providing high efficiency and a relatively stable airflow, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. In contrast, forward curve fan wheels have blades that curve in the direction of rotation, effectively generating high airflow at lower pressures, ideal for HVAC systems and general ventilation. Both types cater to varying performance requirements.</p></p>
